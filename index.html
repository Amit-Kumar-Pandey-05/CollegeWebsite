    About Us

    GOVERNMENT POLYTECHNIC RANCHI is the oldest polytechnic in Jharkhand. It is established in 1935. It was known as Ranchi School Of Engineering. The institute was started way back in 1954 in the summer and Industrial capital of Bihar. Before 1954 this institute was known as Engineering school. It is the best institute for DIPLOMA in jharkhand as it is a root of well education and excellent facilities. It is approved by A.I.C.T.E & It is certified by S.B.T.E, Jharkhand. It is the best for job placement. Many companies for various branches come for the campus selection and choose there appropriate candidates having good skills.

    Government Polytechnic Ranchi has a wealth of talent and knowledge with qualified faculty and high merit students. Equipped with good physical facilities in the form of buildings, lecture halls, laboratories, workshops, hostels etc. with skilled technicians for adoption of appropriate technologies, we consistently adapt to the changing needs through continuous up gradation of all our resources. Our organisation is committed towards imparting education which as defined by Swami Vivekananda says "Education should be man-making, life giving and character building". Our goal as educators is to support all students academically and socially. We make every effort in making every student successful and literate members of society and be able to succeed in the increasingly complex world in which they live and work.
